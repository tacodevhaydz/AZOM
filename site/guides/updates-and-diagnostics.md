The **About** tab in the AZOM panel carries the version, project links, the update notifier
and the diagnostics tools — worth knowing for staying current and for reporting problems.

![The AZOM About tab showing version, update channel and serial traffic capture](/docs/images/AboutPage.png)

## Updates

Turn on automatic checks and pick a **Release channel**. *Development* gets the newest
in-progress builds.

## Serial Traffic Capture

Serial Traffic Capture records every TX/RX frame in memory. If you hit a bug, start a
capture, reproduce the issue, then **Export Bundle (ZIP)** and attach it to a report on
[Discord](https://discord.gg/J4enw43e62) or GitHub. Nothing is saved between launches
unless you export it.
